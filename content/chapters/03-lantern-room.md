The house had one lit window.

Inside, lanterns hung at different heights, each flame turned low as if
to conserve a long, uncertain night. She moved through the room and
watched the shadows rearrange themselves around her.

"You came," said a voice from the far wall.

She set the ledger on the nearest table. "I came to ask what happens
when the story refuses to end."
