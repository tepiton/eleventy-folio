The orchard looked abandoned until she noticed the ribbons: faded
strips tied to branches, each marked with a single name.

Some were names she recognized from childhood stories. Some were
hers, repeated in different hands.

At the center stood a table with one chair. On the table lay a ledger,
open to a blank page, and a pen that had already begun to dry.
